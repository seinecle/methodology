Thesis methodology

[work in progress, come back often for new material!]

This content is designed by Clement Levallois, Associate Professor at emlyon business school.

The targeted audience are students in Master degrees in business schools but more generally any graduate student in the social sciences who is embarking on a thesis to complete their degree.

The material presented here is not intended to replace any content or instruction you received in the methodology classes you already follow, but as a complement to them.
